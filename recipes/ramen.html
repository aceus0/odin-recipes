Ramen

Description

Shin Ramen is a classic take on Ramen, straight from South Korea!

This recipe is quick and delicious! You'll be eating ramen in no time!

Ingredients

  • Shin Ramen Noodle Cup
  • Water

Steps

  1. Slightly open the top lid, fill water to line in cup and pour in provided spice package and veggies.
  2. Put in microwave for 3 minutes, then take out and enjoy!
